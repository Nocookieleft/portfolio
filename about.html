Since I was a child, I've been interested in video games. I grew up loving games like Shuffle Puck Cafe, Load Runner 2, 7th Guest, Myst, Full Throttle, Lemmings, Fury of the Furries... the list goes on. While in primary school, my friend and I spend a lot of time on puzzles and creative activities. We worked out how to fit eight queens on a chessboard in a fashion that no queen would threathen another; We drew short comics about our classmates; We designed levels for Load Runner to implement later on. Games inspired and challenged me. Therefore, it is my dream to work on games that would bring people together to cooporate or just enjoy eachother's company.

Do you need help with a project? Do you want advice regarding serious games? Just want to talk about game related business? Contact me!
